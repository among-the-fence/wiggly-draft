WAR DOG HUNTSMAN
Like hounds on the scent, Huntsmen often pursue hulking enemies,
hunting them down as prey. Their daemonbreath spears melt through
the thickest armour while chaintalons are employed to drive deeply
into gushing innards, tearing through organs and crew alike.
