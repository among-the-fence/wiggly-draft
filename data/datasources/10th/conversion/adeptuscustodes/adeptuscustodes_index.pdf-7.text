CUSTODIAN GUARD
These warriors form the backbone of the shield companies, and every
one is a terrifying force of destruction equal to the strongest of the
foe. Whether armed with guardian spear or sentinel blade, their shots
are perfectly placed, and every cut, thrust and parry is a masterclass
in bladesmanship.
