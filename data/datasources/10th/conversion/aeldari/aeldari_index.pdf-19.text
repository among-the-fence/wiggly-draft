CORSAIR VOIDREAVERS
The skilled and agile warriors known as Voidreavers make up the bulk
of most Corsair warbands. With precise volleys of lethal shuriken fire
they can tear through enemy infantry, or draw their power swords and
storm hostile positions to engage in furious hand-to-hand fighting.
