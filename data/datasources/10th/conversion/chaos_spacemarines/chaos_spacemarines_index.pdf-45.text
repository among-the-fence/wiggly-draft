CULTIST MOB
Chaos Cultists are the mortal devotees of the Dark Gods, ordinary men
and women fallen to promises of temporal power who take to battle in
huge numbers. Bearing a motley collection of improvised, stolen and
makeshift weapons, they can overwhelm even determined enemies
with sheer weight of numbers.
