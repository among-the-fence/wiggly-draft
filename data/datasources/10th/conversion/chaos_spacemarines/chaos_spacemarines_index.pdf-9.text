HAARKEN WORLDCLAIMER
Haarken is the Herald of the Apocalypse and the mouthpiece of
Abaddon. He declares his master’s dominion by driving his daemon
touched Helspear into soon-to-be-conquered worlds, following up with
vicious destruction as he leads hosts of Raptors in strikes that sow
fear and confusion.
