EXALTED SORCERER ON DISC OF TZEENTCH
Only the greatest wielders of esoteric power are said to be granted one
of Tzeentch’s daemonic steeds. Exalted Sorcerers ride these soaring
Discs on plumes of arcane flame while they manipulate coruscating
energy in twisting firestorms.
