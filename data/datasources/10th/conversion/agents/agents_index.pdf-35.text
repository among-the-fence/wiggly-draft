INQUISITORIAL HENCHMEN
To aid them in their esoteric and complex missions, many Inquisitors
maintain retinues of select individuals of rare talent, skill and power.
Motley collections of fighters, fanatics, savants and psykers, they
work ceaselessly in the shadows to protect the Imperium.
