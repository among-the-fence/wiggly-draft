SUBDUCTOR SQUAD
Subductors possess one of the most recognisable – and
feared – silhouettes to the Imperium’s criminal classes. Bearing
Subjugation-pattern assault shields and crackling shock mauls,
they are brutal front-line brawlers capable of crushing the most
stubborn dissent.
