VIGILANT SQUAD
Vigilants brutally sweep the guilty from existence with the signature
combat shotgun of the Adeptus Arbites. These grim Arbitrators are ever
alert to malfeasance and unwavering in its punishment, unleashing
devastating blasts at close range.
