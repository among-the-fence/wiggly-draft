CALLIDUS ASSASSIN
Anarchy flourishes in the wake of a Callidus Assassin’s deployment.
They use the shape-shifting drug polymorphine to insinuate
themselves close to their target, sabotaging systems and seeding
misinformation before closing on their victim with blasts of their
mind-destroying sidearm and flicks of their mysterious blade.
