LEMAN RUSS VANQUISHER
No other battle tank of the Imperial Guard possesses more raw
stopping power than the Leman Russ Vanquisher. Often deployed
against heavily armoured enemies, the long range and high first-hit
kill ratio of its main armament make it the anti-tank weapon of choice
for many commanders.
