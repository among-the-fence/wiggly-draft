LEMAN RUSS PUNISHER
The Punisher variant of the Leman Russ holds a dreaded reputation for
mowing down enemy infantry in vast quantities. Its turret-mounted
multi-barrelled cannon is an unsubtle sprayer of solid ammunition
that applies the maxim of overwhelming firepower to overcome hordes
of foes.
