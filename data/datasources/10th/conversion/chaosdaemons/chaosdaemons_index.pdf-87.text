THE MASQUE OF SLAANESH
Once high in the favour of the Dark Prince, this daemon was cursed
by its petulant deity to dance eternally across reality and beyond for
an imagined slight. Its endless acrobatic performance beguiles and
bewilders, drawing those who witness it into joining the revels even at
the expense of their own survival.
