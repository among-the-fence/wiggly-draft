BROTHER-CAPTAIN
Amongst the Chapter’s foremost combatants, a Brother-Captain
has proven his worth as a leader of great warriors and a martial
exemplar. His place is at the heart of the fight, standing beside
his battle-brothers and focusing their blows by means of psychic
communion even amidst the din of hellish war.
