CYBERWOLF
If critically injured, the largest and most ferocious Fenrisian Wolves
are rebuilt, mechanically augmented by the Chapter’s Iron Priests.
These Cyberwolves may return to their old pack, to lead their kin and
hunt with bionically enhanced muscles and steel fangs. Exhibiting
such strength, flesh-and-blood wolves instinctively follow them.
