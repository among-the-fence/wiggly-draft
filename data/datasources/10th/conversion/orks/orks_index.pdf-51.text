GRETCHIN
What Gretchin – also called grots – lack in stature, courage, resilience,
discipline, motivation, loyalty, aggression and personal hygiene, they
allegedly make up for in numbers. They are at least reasonable shots
with their underpowered firearms, and if all else fails, the Orks find that
grots make excellent bullet-stops.
