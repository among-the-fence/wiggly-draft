KNIGHT ERRANT
These aggressive, mid- to close-range assault Knights excel at
hunting enemy tanks and monsters thanks to their powerful thermal
cannons, reaper chainswords and thunderstrike gauntlets, and
typically plunge into the fiercest fighting.
